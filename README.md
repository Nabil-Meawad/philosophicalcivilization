# Philosophical Civilization

A long-term philosophical research project exploring how consciousness constructs centers of meaning, discovers their limits, and returns to constructing them again.

The project does not begin from a completed philosophical system, nor from final answers.

It begins from questions that may require a long time to examine.

## Foundational Notes

The *Foundational Notes* constitute the methodological and conceptual entry point of the project.

They do not establish immutable principles or final truths.

Their role is to identify phenomena, formulate problems, define questions, and open spaces for inquiry.

The project does not ask its hypotheses to survive.

It asks its method to deserve survival.

Readers interested in the origins of the inquiry are encouraged to begin with the [Foundational Notes](./Foundational%20Note/) series.

## Guiding Principles

* Phenomenon before idea.
* Questions before answers.
* Method before conclusions.
* Criticism before certainty.
* Revision before attachment.

## Project Status

The project is under continuous development.

No document in this repository should be treated as immune from criticism, revision, or abandonment.
