## Overview

PhilosophicalCivilization is an open, evolving civilizational framework that treats civilization not as a historical sequence, but as a structured system of relations, meanings, and conceptual layers.

At its core, the project operates through a central model:

> **CSP — Civilizational Structural Paradigm**

CSP does not function as a doctrine, but as a structural lens for analyzing civilization as a multi-layered system of interaction, meaning, and transformation.

---

A critical framework for examining thought, difference, and civilizational interaction.

## Structural Principle

This project is based on a single assumption:

> Civilization is not a sequence of events, but a structured system of interacting distances.

Meaning emerges not from isolation of elements, but from their relations within structured separation and integration.
